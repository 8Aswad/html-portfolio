The Best Movies According to JABOR

My top 10 movies of all-time.

Dune: Part Two (2024)

A visually stunning sci-fi epic. Incredible world-building and storytelling.

Oppenheimer (2023)

Masterpiece by christopher Nolan. Intense, emotional, and brilliantly acted.

Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse (2023)

Beautifully animated. A groundbreaking superhero film with heart and style.

Everything Everywhere All at once (2022)

Wild, emotional, and creative multiverse adventure. Totally unique.

The Batman (2022)

A darker and more grounded take on Batman. Cinematic and stylish.

Top Gun: Maverick (2022)

Thrilling and nostalgic. one of the best action films of recent times.

Avatar: The way of Water (2022)

Visually breathtaking. James Cameron pushes the Limits of CGI again.

Killers of the Flower Moon (2023)

Martin Scorsese's historical epic. Emotional and powerful storytelling.

John Wick: Chapter 4 (2023)

Stylish and action-packed. The best of the John Wick Series.

Mission: Impossible - Dead Reckoning Part One (2023)

Tom Cruise delivers another intense spy thriller with jaw-dropping stunts.
